Number of times each pair occurs:
(click or tap a column header to sort)

For Frostbite's 9 stages, there are [(9 * 9) - 9] / 2 = 36 possible pairings of different stages. For a uniform distribution (like randomly picking), we would expect each pairing to occur 100% / 36 = 2.78% of the time, on average. The total number of ban pairs recorded is 577. By dividing the above table by 577, we can see what each pair's actual % of occurences is in reality. Finally, that real % occurence is divided by 2.78% for a ratio of how many times higher or lower it is than the uniform average.

About The Stats & This Webpage
  • At Genesis 6 and Frostbite 2019, two "major" sized tournaments, competitors during pools were asked by pool captains to optionally fill out a short survey sheet.
  • Original source: the stage surveys were coordinated by "Jaaahsh" (Josh). He published the survey results as these Google spreadsheets: Genesis 6 and Frostbite 2019. More info about the surveys can be found in these spreadsheets.
  • The main purpose of this webpage is to provide visualizations. I make no commentary here. To find analyses or alternative reports of this same data, see the following links:
    • Josh's twitter thread for Genesis 6 data
    • Reddit discussion thread for Josh's release of Genesis 6 data
    • PracticalTAS's twitter thread for Genesis 6 data
    • PracticalTAS's reddit thread for Genesis 6 data
    • PracticalTAS's twitter thread for Frostbite 2019 data
  • I am not affiliated with the tournament organizers (TOs), or any official orgs.
