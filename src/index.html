About The Stage Data
  • At Genesis 6 and Frostbite 2019, two "major" sized tournaments, competitors during pools were asked by pool captains to voluntarily fill out a short survey sheet.
  • Original source: the stage surveys were coordinated by "Jaaahsh" (Josh). He published the survey results as these Google spreadsheets: Genesis 6 and Frostbite 2019. More info about the surveys can be found in these spreadsheets.
  • Genesis 6's rules can be found here. Frostbite 2019's rules can be found here. The main differences in stages are: Frostbite does not use Unova or Castle Siege at all, and Town & City takes Lylat's place as a starter.
